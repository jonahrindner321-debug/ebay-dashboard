Data Deletion Policy

Sellers and clients can request disconnection and deletion of locally stored Seller OS data.

Last updated: April 25, 2026

What Can Be Deleted

Upon a verified request, Seller OS can delete locally stored OAuth tokens, platform connection records, account mappings, and dashboard records associated with a connected store/client account.

What Seller OS Cannot Delete

Seller OS is read-only for TikTok Shop and does not directly modify TikTok Shop records. If a seller needs deletion or correction inside TikTok Shop itself, the seller should use TikTok Shop tools or contact TikTok Shop support.

How To Request Deletion

Contact the Seller OS administrator using the email in the TikTok Shop Partner Center registration or the contact page. Include the store/client name and the platform account to disconnect.

Timing

Seller OS will make commercially reasonable efforts to process verified deletion requests promptly, subject to backup, legal, security, or operational retention requirements.
