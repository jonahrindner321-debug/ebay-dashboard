Terms of Use

These terms apply to use of Seller OS as a custom ecommerce operations dashboard.

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Use of Seller OS

Seller OS provides dashboards and reporting for authorized ecommerce stores. Users are responsible for ensuring they have permission to connect and view each store/account.

Read-Only Platform Access

Seller OS is designed to use read-only platform access where supported. It does not perform seller account automation, order updates, buyer messaging, fulfillment actions, or listing modifications.

Account Authorization

Connected seller accounts must be authorized by the seller or an authorized representative. Users must not connect accounts they do not have permission to access.

No Financial, Legal, or Tax Advice

Seller OS displays operational and performance data. It does not provide legal, tax, accounting, or financial advice.

Availability

Seller OS is an evolving internal/custom dashboard. Features, integrations, and reports may change as the product develops.
