🔑 One-time setup

This dashboard needs a free Google API key to pull live data from each eBay sheet and auto-detect new monthly tabs.

  1. Go to console.cloud.google.com
  2. Create / select a project → APIs & Services → Enable APIs
  3. Enable the Google Sheets API
  4. Go to Credentials → Create API Key
  5. Paste it below — saved only in your browser

Your key is stored in localStorage and only sent to Google's servers.

📖 Understanding Your Data ▼
📊 Core Metrics
Revenue
The sale price of each item — what the buyer paid before any fees or costs are deducted.
Profit
What you actually keep after subtracting your cost of goods and eBay fees from revenue.
Revenue − Cost − Fees
ROI
Return on Investment — how much profit you made relative to what you spent sourcing the item.
Profit ÷ Cost × 100
Margin
Profit as a percentage of revenue. Shows how much of each sale dollar you keep.
Profit ÷ Revenue × 100
Avg Sale
Average revenue per transaction. Higher is generally better — means you're moving pricier items.
🏅 Health Scores
What is the Health Score?
A 0–100 score showing how well an account is performing overall. Made up of three components.
ROI Component (40 pts)
Higher ROI earns more points. Maxes out at 100% ROI. Rewards efficient sourcing.
Activity Component (30 pts)
How many days the account sold vs. total days since its first ever sale. Rewards consistency.
Profitable Days (30 pts)
Percentage of active days where profit was positive. Rewards reliable positive days.
Grades
A = 80–100 · B = 60–79 · C = 40–59 · D = 0–39
💸 Profit Splits
Owned Accounts
Accounts owned by J&R. Profit splits 60% to J&R and 40% to Danian.
J&R 60% · Danian 40%
Jacob Accounts
Accounts run by Jacob as operator. Profit splits three ways.
J&R 50% · Jacob 10% · Danian 40%
Partner Accounts
Accounts run by external partners. Partners take the largest share.
J&R 20% · Danian 30% · Partner 50%
⚠️ Inactive Badge
Shown on health cards when an account hasn't had a sale in 5+ days. The number shows how many days since the last sale.
📈 Charts & Trends
Momentum Chart
Shows your 7-day rolling average profit. Smooths out day-to-day noise to reveal the real trend direction.
Heatmap
A calendar view of daily profit. Darker green = more profit. Red = loss day. Helps spot weekly patterns.
Day of Week Chart
Average profit per weekday across all history. Use this to identify your strongest and weakest days.
Month Comparison
Side-by-side monthly cards. Arrows show % change vs the prior month. "In Progress" means the current month isn't over yet.
Projection
Estimates end-of-month totals based on your daily average so far this month — only shown when the month is still active.
