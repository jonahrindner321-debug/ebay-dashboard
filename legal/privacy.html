Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Seller OS handles data for ecommerce sellers, operators, and clients who use the dashboard.

Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. What Seller OS Does

Seller OS is a custom ecommerce operations dashboard. It connects to authorized seller accounts and data sources to display read-only performance metrics, including orders, product/listing information, finance/reporting data, and operational summaries.

Seller OS does not automate seller accounts, scrape login sessions, control storefronts, update listings, update orders, message buyers, or perform write actions through connected platform APIs.

2. Information We Process

Depending on the connected account and approved scopes, Seller OS may process:

  • Seller account identifiers, store names, and client/store mappings.
  • Order, revenue, product, listing, finance, and reporting information.
  • OAuth authorization tokens and token metadata needed to maintain the connection.
  • Dashboard usage and operational records needed to support the service.

3. How Data Is Used

Seller OS uses connected data to provide dashboards, reporting, operational visibility, client views, and account-level insights. Data is used only for the seller/client account it belongs to and is not sold.

4. Authorization and Access

Where a platform supports OAuth, each seller authorizes Seller OS directly. TikTok Shop access is configured using approved read-only scopes. Access is isolated by store/client so users should only see data associated with their authorized account.

5. Data Storage and Security

Seller OS is hosted on managed cloud infrastructure. Production secrets are stored outside the codebase, OAuth tokens are encrypted before storage, and dashboard traffic is served over HTTPS.

6. Data Sharing

Seller OS does not sell personal data. Data may be processed by infrastructure providers used to host the application, database, and serverless functions. Data may also be disclosed if required by law or to protect the security and integrity of Seller OS.

7. Data Deletion

Sellers may request disconnection and deletion of locally stored Seller OS records. See the Data Deletion Policy.

8. Contact

For privacy requests, contact the Seller OS administrator through the email used in the TikTok Shop Partner Center registration or through the contact page.
