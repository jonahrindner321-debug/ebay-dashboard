Last updated: April 25, 2026

How To Contact Seller OS

Contact the Seller OS administrator using the contact email listed in the TikTok Shop Partner Center registration for this app.

Include the connected store name, platform account, request type, and a way to verify that you are authorized to make the request.

Request Types

  • Disconnect a TikTok Shop or ecommerce platform account.
  • Delete locally stored Seller OS dashboard records.
  • Report suspected unauthorized access.
  • Request information about Seller OS privacy or security controls.
